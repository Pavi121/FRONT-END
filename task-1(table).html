Chocolate Chip Cookies


Prep Time:15 minutes

Cook Time:10 minutes

STEPS

  1. Preheat the over to 350oF(180o)C.
  2. Mix flour and sugar in a bowel
  3. Add chocolate chips and stir well
  4. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown

Ingredients

Ingredients Quality
Flour 2 Cups
Sugar 1 Cups
Chocolate chips 1 Cups

Enjoy your HomeMade Cookies
